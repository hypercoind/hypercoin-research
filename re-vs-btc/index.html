This calculator helps you compare three common wealth-building strategies:

  • Real Estate Investment: Buying property with a mortgage and building equity over time
  • Portfolio Investment: Investing a lump sum across diversified assets (Bitcoin, stocks, bonds, savings)
  • Bitcoin Savings Strategy: Using monthly rent vs. mortgage savings to dollar-cost average into Bitcoin

ü§î Common Decision Scenarios

Scenario 1: "Should I save for a down payment or invest in Bitcoin?"

How to use: Set the property price to your target home, adjust the down payment to your savings goal, and compare the real estate investment with the Bitcoin savings strategy results.

Key insight: See whether renting and investing your would-be mortgage payments in Bitcoin outperforms buying property.

Scenario 2: "I have $100K. Should I put it toward a house or invest it?"

How to use: Set your available cash as both the down payment and initial portfolio investment. Compare real estate returns with the diversified portfolio results.

Key insight: Compare the leveraged real estate investment against a balanced portfolio approach.

Scenario 3: "I'm renting and have extra cash flow. What should I do with it?"

How to use: Enter your actual monthly rent in the "Current Rent & Savings" section. The calculator will compute your monthly savings by renting vs. buying and show how investing those savings in Bitcoin could compound over time.

Key insight: Discover whether your rent savings, when invested consistently, could outpace property ownership returns based on your real rent costs.

üìä Key Assumptions & Methodology

Property Appreciation: 4% annually (US historical average)
Rent Inflation: User configurable (default 3.5% annually)
Bitcoin Growth: Modeled with decreasing volatility over time as the market matures
Portfolio Returns: Based on historical asset class performance
Tax Considerations: Not included - consult a tax professional for your specific situation
Monthly Savings: Calculated as the difference between mortgage payments (PITI+PMI+HOA) and your actual rent input
